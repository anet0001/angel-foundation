OUR SERVICES

At The Angels Foundation, we are committed to supporting newcomers as they embark on their journey in a new community. As a non-profit organization, our mission is to facilitate a smooth transition by assisting with job searches, providing local cultural food to ease dietary adjustments, and encouraging community volunteering. We also offer vital information on free government services available locally, ensuring that newcomers have the resources they need. Our goal is to create a supportive network that helps individuals adjust, thrive, and make meaningful connections. Together, we make integration both smooth and enriching! Anti raising workshops

Job Search Support

We're dedicated to supporting newcomers in our community by providing essential job search resources and guidance. Through workshops and mentorship, we empower individuals to navigate the local job market with confidence. Together, we build connections and foster a sense of belonging for all!

Food Services

Our organization is dedicated to helping newcomers settle in by offering traditional nutritional meals that celebrate their heritage. By nourishing both body and spirit, we foster a sense of belonging and create a welcoming community for all.
